Key Features

Crop Disease Detection

Leveraging advanced technology such as image processing and machine learning algorithms to detect and diagnose crop diseases accurately and promptly. This functionality enables farmers to identify potential threats early, facilitating timely intervention and minimizing yield losses.

Chat Feature

Providing a user-friendly chat interface to facilitate communication among farmers, agricultural experts, vendors, and other stakeholders. Through real-time interaction, users can seek advice, share experiences, and exchange information on farming practices, crop management, and market trends.

Geo Locator

Integrating a geolocation feature to help users locate nearby vendors, agricultural service providers, and fellow farmers. By leveraging GPS technology, Farmer Buddy Marketplace with Fertilizer Store enables seamless connections within the agricultural community, fostering trade, knowledge sharing, and community engagement.

Voice-to-Text

Incorporating a voice-to-text feature to allow farmers to input text hands-free using voice commands. This functionality enhances usability, particularly for farmers working in the field or those with limited typing abilities, enabling them to access information and communicate more efficiently.

Marketplace

Establishing an online marketplace within the platform where farmers can buy and sell agricultural products, equipment, and services. This feature promotes fair trade practices, enhances market access, and fosters economic growth within rural communities.

Multilabel Classification

Implementing advanced multilabel classification algorithms to categorize and classify agricultural products, services, and information accurately. This functionality enhances search capabilities within the marketplace, enabling farmers to find relevant products and services efficiently based on multiple criteria.
